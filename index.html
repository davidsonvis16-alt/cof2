Our Story

Six years in Westlands

We started with a simple idea: that coffee shouldn't just be an energy hit, it should be a brief ritual to center yourself in the middle of a busy Nairobi day.

Our beans are sourced directly from sustainable farms in Nyeri and Kiambu, roasted micro-batch weekly, and served alongside fresh sourdough pastries baked before the sun comes up.
