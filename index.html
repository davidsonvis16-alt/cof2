Our story

Six years, one gate off Muthithi Road.

SipSpell started as a single espresso machine and a handful of hand-painted menu boards. Six years later, it's the same brushstroke handwriting — just more of it: iced beverages for the Nairobi heat, dessert boxes for the drive-home crowd, and a pup cup for the dog waiting patiently by the door.

Every drink on the menu is still named the way the first one was — a little playful, a little handwritten, never generic.
